    • DIY Acai Bowl

    Ingredients

    Acai Puree

    • 1 packet of Sambazon Unsweeted Acai Smoothie Pack
    • 1/2 medium banana
    • 1/2 cup frozen mixed berries
    • 1/2 cups unsweetened vanilla almond milk

    Toppings

    • 1/2 medium banana
    • 2 tbs coconut shavings
    • 1/4 cup ancient grains cheerios
    • 1 tsp chia seeds

    Instructions

    1. Before opening your acai pack, run it under water for 5 seconds to thaw
    2. Grab a blender and add your acai smoothie packet, banana, mixed berries, and milk. Blend together
    3. Add your toppings, get creative! Turn it into your own beautiful masterpiece
    4. Enjoy! There is nothing better than an acai bowl to start off a stellar day
