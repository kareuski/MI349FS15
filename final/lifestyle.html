    Tips To Help You Get On Your Mat Everyday

    • 1.) Go with a friend! Everything is better with your best friend, right?
    • 2.) Track your progress. Take pictures, write down how you feel afterwards. What poses were easy for you, do you feel you get better sleep after yoga?
    • 3.) Practice postures that are difficult for you. Motivate yourself into wanting to be able to achieve that dancer pose that almost seems impossible.
    • 4.) Switch up your routine! Try a new sequence, test out a new instructor. It's always great to meet new people and expand yourself into other types of practice.
    • 5.) Most importantly, reward yourself. Celebrate your successes when you achieve a goal.
