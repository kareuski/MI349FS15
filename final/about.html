My name is Kirsten Kareus, I am 21 years old born and raised in the Mitten state. I found yoga about five years ago. A doctor had requested if I started to practice yoga everyday for the rest of my life it will help strengthen my core in order to reduce back pain from severe scoliosis. Not only had yoga helped me in that aspect, I also had found a passion to better my mind, body and soul through the lifestyle of yoga. Your body is your temple and giving it the proper nourishment is truly important for a happy and healthy soul. I am not a professional, however, I enjoy helping others discover wholesome happiness and here is a place where I will post my personal tips and tricks.
